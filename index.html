This. Is. Condution.

A personal project management system that makes sense and will not burn your pockets out.

The App

Condution is a wonderful electron app built to create a perfectly easy task-management dashboard that’s powerful, yet also simple. The inspiration of the project spanned from our long frustration with the current state of productivity software on the internet — they are expensive and often complicated to use. Condution attempts to change this. We want to create an open-source productivity/task-management app that is both simple, and, well, free (but we do take Patreon donations though).
